About

Patrizia - Born in Rome, Italy.

Graduated at the Michelangelo Institute and studied at the Universita' di Roma. Patrizia moved to California in her twenties, she's married, has two children and is a Livermore resident.

In her "Monna Luna Art Studio", she crafted Tiffany style panels, boxes and kaleidoscopes. Over the years, her art has evolved along with the different stages of her life.

Patrizia is renowned in the Livermore Wine Country for her creative mosaics and has shown them at several local art galleries. Holiday showings have been held at "The Big White House" and the "Bent Creek" wineries in Livermore. Her work has been mentioned in local newspapers and was featured on the cover of the "Independent".

She currently creates large mosaics and fused glass jewelry.

Monna Luna is the equivalent of Lady Moon in the Italian spoken during the Renaissance.
